Welcome to ACCFB's Annual Civil Rights Training

Alameda County Community Food Bank is proud to present this annual Civil Rights Training. Thank you for making time for this important course! This training is so important, the Federal Government requires that all TEFAP agencies complete an approved Civil Rights training program every year. We hope you find it helpful and beneficial!


Instructions

To complete this course, watch the course video and complete the Check Your Understanding quiz below.
Important!!

To receive credit for this course, you must fill out and return the course Certificate of Completion. The Certificate of Completion will be emailed to you along with instructions for completing and returning it. A passing score of 80% or higher on the Check Your Understanding quiz is required to receive a Certificate of Completion.


Course Video

Click the play button to start the video.



Check Your Understanding

Answer the quiz questions below, then click Submit. A passing score of 80% is required for you to receive credit for this course, and you have an unlimited number of attempts. Your responses will be saved. If you need to re-take the quiz, simply click your browser's refresh button to edit your original answers, then click Submit to update your responses and see your new score.
